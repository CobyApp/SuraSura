SuraSura is a real-time voice recognition and translation app that lets you communicate effortlessly across language barriers.

Simply tap the mic, speak, and your words are instantly recognized and translated into the language of your choice. Perfect for travel, business meetings, and language learning.

Features:
• Real-time voice recognition — converts speech to text on the fly
• Instant translation — translates recognized text in seconds
• Face-to-Face mode — point the screen at your conversation partner for natural two-way dialogue
• Auto text-to-speech — translated results read aloud automatically
• Dozens of languages supported
• Dark and Light mode
• Clean, intuitive interface

SuraSura helps you connect with anyone, anywhere — no language skills required.
