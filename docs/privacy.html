Overview

SuraSura is a real-time voice translation app developed by Coby (doyoung@minami-hd.co.jp). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Do Not Collect

SuraSura does not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit your voice recordings to our servers
  • Create user accounts or require registration
  • Track your location
  • Share any personal data with third parties for advertising
  • Use analytics SDKs or ad networks

Microphone & Speech Recognition

The App uses Apple's on-device Speech Recognition framework. Audio may be processed by Apple's servers under Apple's own privacy policy (apple.com/legal/privacy). SuraSura never receives or stores your raw audio.

Translation

Text translation uses Apple's Translation framework. SuraSura does not log or retain translated text beyond your current session.

Text-to-Speech (TTS)

Playback uses Apple's AVSpeechSynthesizer, which runs entirely on-device. No data leaves your device for this feature.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Effective date: March 2025
